TISI Chat

TISI Chat is a multifunctional messaging program I made as final project for high school together with Geoffrey De Belie. For this, we've received the price for best final project of the year!

Features
  • Group and private chatting
  • Rich content messaging (Graphical emoticons, BB Code markup)
  • Spam and profanity filters
  • Different ranks with abilities (Moderators can kick people, Administrators can also ban)
